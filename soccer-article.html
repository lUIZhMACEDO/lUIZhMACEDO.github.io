Overview

This is an opinion piece I wrote about corruption around the 2022 World Cup in Qatar. I focus on how migrant workers were abused to build the stadiums and infrastructure, and how FIFA officials and local organizers looked the other way while money and influence decided what was supposed to be the biggest celebration in world football.

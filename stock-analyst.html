AI Stock Analyst

Overview

I built this full-stack stock analysis platform that lets anyone enter their portfolio and instantly receive institutional-grade analysis. Users input their stock tickers, share counts, and average costs, then the system pulls live data from multiple financial APIs to generate personalized BUY/SELL/HOLD signals, compute technical indicators, fetch analyst ratings, assess portfolio risk, and deliver a full professional report to their email. The public app shows only the Analyze Stocks flow—visitors enter their own data with no access to any personal portfolio.

Tech Stack

Python Streamlit yfinance Finnhub API Pandas NumPy TA-Lib Google News RSS SMTP / Gmail Streamlit Cloud Plotly

Live Application

Enter your stocks below to try it. Your data stays in your session—no personal portfolios are exposed.

Features

Smart Signals

Per-ticker BUY/SELL/HOLD/TRIM signals based on P&L thresholds, PE ratios, RSI levels, and 52-week positioning.

Technical Indicators

Real-time RSI, MACD, Bollinger Bands, EMA crossovers, ATR-based stop-loss and target levels.

Analyst Ratings

Buy/Hold/Sell counts, consensus price targets, and insider transaction tracking via Finnhub.

Risk Dashboard

Portfolio beta, sector exposure, per-position risk, and 95% Value at Risk (VaR).

Email Reports

Full HTML report delivered to the user's inbox with charts, signals, technicals, and risk data.

Crypto Tab

Fear & Greed Index, live BTC/ETH/SOL/BNB/XRP prices, and technical indicators.

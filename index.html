About Us

Meet the Creator

Hi, I'm Geise Esteves, the creator behind Luz e Aroma. I'm passionate about crafting beautiful, aromatic candles that bring joy and warmth to your home. Each candle is handmade with care and dedication, using a variety of carefully selected fragrances to create the perfect scent for every occasion.

Our Specialty

I specialize in creating personalized candles with custom scents and designs. Whether you're looking for a special gift, want to match your home's decor, or need a unique fragrance combination, I love working with you to create the perfect candle. From seasonal scents to signature fragrances, each piece is crafted with attention to detail and a commitment to quality.
